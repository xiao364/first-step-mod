## Displays a title the **first time** you enter a structure (any structure that can be located with `/locate`). The tracking resets when you leave the world.


https://modrinth.com/mod/first-step-mod


### Supported Structures
Currently works with these mods:  
- Vanilla Minecraft  
- [Terralith](https://modrinth.com/mod/terralith)  
- [qraftyfied: STRUCTURES](https://modrinth.com/mod/qraftyfied)
- [Repurposed Structures](https://modrinth.com/mod/repurposed-structures-fabric) 

*Recommended to use together with this mod: [Modern UI(1.21.9+)](https://modrinth.com/mod/modernui-mc-mvus) or [Modern UI(other versions)](https://modrinth.com/mod/modern-ui/versions) , [Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu)*

### Features
- **Lightweight** – Minimal impact on game performance.
- **Highly Customizable** – You can customize the title color for each structure, dimension color, and mod prefix color by editing the files in the `lang` folder inside the JAR.
- **Multi-language** – More language support will be added in the future (currently Chinese & English).

### Inspiration
Inspired by *[Traveler's Titles](https://modrinth.com/mod/travelers-titles)* and *[What Is This Structure?](https://modrinth.com/mod/wits)* – this mod also hopes to see those mods updated to the latest Minecraft versions.

### Important Notes
- **single only** – does nothing on a server or multiplayer (may try to improve in the future).
- **AI‑generated mod** – please keep in mind:
  1. Bug fixes may be slow.  
  2. I may not be able to solve every issue (though I'll try).  
  3. Performance is not optimized.  
  4. Compatibility with other mods may be limited.  
  5. **You are free to use, modify, and include it in your modpacks** (as long as you're okay with the compatibility/performance caveats).

## **首次**进入某个结构（可通过 `/locate` 命令定位的结构）时显示标题。退出世界后记录会重置。


https://modrinth.com/mod/first-step-mod


### 支持的结构
目前适用于以下模组：  
- 原版 Minecraft  
- [Terralith](https://modrinth.com/datapack/terralith)  
- [qraftyfied: STRUCTURES](https://modrinth.com/datapack/qraftyfied)  
- [Repurposed Structures](https://modrinth.com/mod/repurposed-structures-fabric)  

*建议与本模组一起使用：[Modern UI（1.21.9+）](https://modrinth.com/mod/modernui-mc-mvus) 或 [Modern UI（其他版本）](https://modrinth.com/mod/modern-ui/versions) 、[Mod Menu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu)*

### 特性
- **轻量化** – 对游戏性能影响极小。  
- **高度自定义** – 你可以通过修改 JAR 文件内 `lang` 文件夹中的文件，来自定义每个结构的标题颜色、维度颜色以及模组前缀颜色。  
- **多语言** – 未来会加入更多语言支持（目前支持中文和英文）。

### 灵感来源
灵感来自 *[Traveler's Titles](https://modrinth.com/mod/travelers-titles)* 和 *[What Is This Structure?](https://modrinth.com/mod/wits)* – 本模组也希望这些模组能更新到最新的 Minecraft 版本。

### 重要说明
- **仅单人生效** – 在服务端或多人游戏中没有任何作用（未来可能会尝试改进）。  
- **AI 生成的模组** – 请注意：
  1. 修复 bug 可能会比较慢。  
  2. 我不一定能解决所有问题（但会尽力）。  
  3. 性能优化较差。  
  4. 与其他模组的兼容性可能有限。  
  5. **你可以自由使用、修改并放入你的整合包中**（前提是你能够接受上述兼容性和性能方面的不足）。
